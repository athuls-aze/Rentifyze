Frequently Asked Questions

How does the QR Authentication work?

When you complete a checkout on Rentify, our servers generate a highly encrypted QR payload. You must present this QR code physically to the seller or host at the designated campus meetup location. The seller has strict instructions not to hand over textbooks, lab coats, or provide academic tutelage until you produce the verifiable code.

Who can remove a listing?

To prevent arbitrary data scraping, only the authenticated user session (verified by your logged-in College Email Address) that originally mapped and broadcasted the listing to the Firebase database is permitted to execute the Remove action. If you believe a listing is fraudulent, you must contact campus moderation.

What happens if a rental is damaged?

By entering the Rentify ecosystem, all actors consent to the standard Liability Framework mapped in our Conditions of Use. If you rent high-value lab equipment or aesthetic assets and cause irreparable damage, your college credentials may be permanently banned from the marketplace, and you will be liable for the asset's digital cost.

Is my payment data recorded locally?

Rentify utilizes state-of-the-art tokenized checkout wrappers. We simulate a secure 256-bit SSL pipeline natively across the client instance, meaning no hard credit metrics are permanently written into unprotected Local Storage bounds. Your academic safety is paramount.

Still need help?

Our campus administrators are online from 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM to assist with transaction overrides.
