Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Notice describes how Rentify.com, Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you use our peer-to-peer sharing and rental platform. By using Rentify, you consent to the practices described in this notice.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly to us: We collect your college email address, name, login credentials, and the content of your listings (e.g., items for sale, rental descriptions, and skills offered).

Automatically collected information: We may collect standard technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system when you access our site.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Rentify platform.
  • Facilitate peer-to-peer transactions and communications.
  • Verify your college affiliation for campus safety.
  • Send you technical notices, updates, and support messages.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We only share your information with other platform users as necessary to complete transactions (for example, showing your contact info when you offer a skill), or as required by law.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Notice, please contact privacy@rentify.com.
